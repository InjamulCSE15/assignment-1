About

Ever since Chris Evans landed the role of Captain America in the MCU, fans from around the world have been keeping up with his life constantly. Evans was known before he was cast for the films, but he has truly become one of the industry's most famous names after nearly 10 years of playing Cap. A perfect example of how the MCU helped Evans is that he landed a role in the star-studded cast of Knives Out. It was his first role after Avengers: Endgame.
Throughout the many movies that Evans has been a part of throughout his career, fans have had the opportunity to learn more about him. Although, there's only so much that you can learn about a player through a movie.
